*Some experts suggest using a cut-off of 8 instead of 10 to elevate sensitivity to detect GAD.(Plummer F, 2016)

GAD-7 score ≥ 10 had a sensitivity of 89% and a specificity of 82% for GAD in an initial study. (Spitzer RL, 2006)

The GAD-7 also performs as a screening tool for three other common anxiety disorders — Panic Disorder, Social Anxiety Disorder(social phobia), and Posttraumatic Stress Disorder(PTSD).(Kroenke K, 2007)

Further evaluation (including diagnostic interview and mental status examination) and/or referral to a mental health professional is recommended if score ≥ 8.
